# Tones

There are 4 tones present in mandarin language:
1. High     ->     | ------ |  High voice        Example: ā (straight line above a)  
                   |        |  Medium voice
                   |        |  Low voice

2. Rising   ->     |  /-----|  High voice        Example: á (Raising line above a)
                   |_/      |
                   |        |

3. Low      ->     |        |  High voice        Example: ǎ (Falling from low further and increasing to medium tone)
                   |       /|  Medium voice
                   |-.__.-' |  Low voice

4. Falling  ->     |`\       |  High voice       Example: à (Falling from high tone to low tone)
                   |  `\     |  Medium voicâ
                   |    `\   |  Low voice

Examples: 
1. High tone - mā (Maaaaaahhh)   - Mother
2. Low tone  - mǎ (Mhae eaa aah) - Horse
3. Raising   - má (Mhe aaaaaah)  - Hemp
4. Falling   - mà (Mhae)         - To scold
